62EA: Timer Counters
Read by the routines at Timer_Interrupt and Timer_Display. Updated by the routine at Timer_Reset.
Byte Contents
0 Interrupt counter (decremented each 1/50 seconds)
1 Seconds counter
2 Minutes counter
