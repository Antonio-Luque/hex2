70F7: Put Token on Positions
Used by the routines at BRAINIAC_best_move, Alphabeta_Min, Heuristic_Min, Alphabeta_Max, Heuristic_Max and Main_Match_Loop.
Put a token on both CYAN_POSITIONS and RED_POSITIONS areas.
Input
A $02 (FRIEND) or $7E (ENEMY)
HL CYAN_POSITIONS token address
PathFinder_PutToken 70F7 LD (HL),A put token at cyan position
70F8 INC H move HL to corresponding RED_POSITIONS address
70F9 XOR $7C invert side
70FB LD (HL),A put token at red position
70FC RET
