70FD: Erase Token on Positions
Used by the routines at BRAINIAC_best_move, Alphabeta_Min, Heuristic_Min, Alphabeta_Max and Heuristic_Max.
Erase a token on both CYAN_POSITIONS and RED_POSITIONS areas.
Input
HL CYAN_POSITIONS token address
PathFinder_EraseToken 70FD LD (HL),$00 erase token at cyan position
70FF INC H move HL to corresponding RED_POSITIONS address
7100 LD (HL),$00 erase token at red position
7102 RET
