5CA1: Jump to Timer Interrupt
Initialized by the routine at Main_Entry.
This address belongs to the calculator's memory area and it is pointed by IY+$67 after Spectrum start.
After Main_Entry initialization this address stores the instruction 'JP Timer_Interrupt' and the interrupt vector address located at $2EFF. The value pointed at ROM address $2EFF is $5CA1.
JUMP_INT 5CA1 DEFB $00,$00,$00
