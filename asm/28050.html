6D92: Execute a Delay
Used by the routines at Main_GetPlayer_Move, Draw_Token and Main_Match_End.

Main_Delay_Loop1 6D95 LD B,$00 set inner loop counter (256)
Main_Delay_Loop2 6D97 DJNZ Main_Delay_Loop2 repeat until B=0

6D9A JR NZ,Main_Delay_Loop1 repeat until C=0
